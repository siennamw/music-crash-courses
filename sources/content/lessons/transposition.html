Transposition

Transposition occurs when a segment of music is raised or lowered by a given interval, keeping all the internal intervals the same.

For example, when the pattern of whole and half steps of the C major scale is preserved, but we begin on C-sharp instead of C, we have transposed the C major scale into a C-sharp major scale.
